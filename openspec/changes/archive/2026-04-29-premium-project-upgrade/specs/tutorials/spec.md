## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Tutorial series exists
The project SHALL have a comprehensive tutorial series.

#### Scenario: 10 tutorials available
- **WHEN** a learner visits docs/guide/tutorials/
- **THEN** they find 10 step-by-step tutorials

#### Scenario: Progressive difficulty
- **WHEN** a learner follows tutorials
- **THEN** difficulty increases progressively

#### Scenario: Practical examples
- **WHEN** a learner reads a tutorial
- **THEN** they find practical, runnable examples
